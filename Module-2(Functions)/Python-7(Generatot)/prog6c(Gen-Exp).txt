Generator  expression
--------------------------
1) What  is  another  way  to  implement  a  generator ?  --->  With  generator  expression

2) How  to  define  a  generator  expression ?  --->  (expression  for  loop)

3) g = (x * x   for   x    in  range(5))
    What  is  the  expression  called ? ---> Generator  expression  but  not  tuple  comprehension

4) g = (x * x   for   x    in  range(5))
    What  does  the  statement  do ?  --->
							Creates  an  empty  generator  object  but  does  not  store  5  elements  in  generator

5) Why  are  0 ^ 2 , 1 ^ 2 , 2 ^ 2 , 3 ^ 2 , 4 ^ 2  not  stored  in  generator  ?  --->  
											Since  elements  are  yielded  on  demand  but  not  in  advance

6) In  other  words,  generator  is  an  empty  object
