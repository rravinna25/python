Different  forms  of   *  operator
---------------------------------------
1) What  is  the  role  of  *   operator  in  10 * 20  ?  --->  Multiplication

2) What  is  the  role  of  *   operator  in  *object  ?  --->  Unpacks  object  to  elements

3) What  is  the  role  of  *   operator  in  sequence *  3  ?  --->  Repeats  sequence  thrice

4) def   f1(* , a , b): 
            pass
   What  is  the  role  of  *   operator ?  --->  Ensures  function  expects  KA's  only

5) def  f1(*a): 
           pass
     What  is  the  role  of  *  operator ?  ---> Var-arg  parameter 
								       i.e.  Any  number  of  arguments  can  be  passed  to  f1()  function

6) Finally  *  operator  is  overloaded  in  python
