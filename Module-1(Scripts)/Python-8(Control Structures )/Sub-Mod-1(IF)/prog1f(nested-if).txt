Nested  if
------------
1) if   cond1:    
	  if  cond2:	  
		stmt1
		stmt2
		stmt3	  
          else:   	  
		stmt4
		stmt5
		stmt6
   else:    
	  if  cond3:	  
		stmt7
		stmt8
		stmt9	  
          else:   	  
		stmt10
		stmt11
		stmt12           
    stmt13

2) What  is  nested  if ?  ---> if  within  another  if

3) When  are  statements  1 ,  2  and  3  executed  ?  ---> When  both  cond1  and  cond2  are  true
     When  are  statements  4 ,  5  and  6  executed  ?  --->When  cond1  is  True  and  cond2  is  false
     When  are  statements  7 ,  8  and  9  executed  ?  --->  When  cond1  is  false  and  cond3  is  true
     When  are  statements  10 ,  11  and  12  executed  ?  ---> When  both  cond1  and  cond3  are  false

4) When  is  statement13  executed ? --->   Always  executed  irrespective  of  the  conditions  becoz  it  is  outside  if
