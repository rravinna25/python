Slice
------
1) What  is  obtained  when  string  is  sliced ?   --->  A  new  string  with  sliced  characters  (or)  desired  characters

2) What  is  the  syntax  of  slice ?  --->  string[begin : end : step]

3) string[begin : end : 0]
    Is  the  statement  valid ?  ---> No  becoz  step  cannot  be  0

4) In  other  words,  step  can  be  positive (or)  negative  but  not  0

5) string[x : y : z]  
    What  is  obatined ?  --->  String  from  indexes  x  to  y - 1  in  steps  of  z

6) string[x : y : -z]  
    What  is  obatined ?  --->  String  from  indexes  x  to  y + 1  in  steps  of  -z

7) string[begin : end]
    What  is  the  default  step ?  ---> 1

8) string[ : : +ve  step] 
    What  is  the  default  begin  and  why  ?  --->  0  becoz  index  of  1st  character  is  0
    What  is  the  default  end  and  why  ?  --->  String  length  becoz  index  of  last  char  is  length -  1

9) string[  :  :  -ve step] 
    What  is  the  default  begin  and  why ?  --->  -1  becoz  index  of  first  character  is  -1  from  right  to  left
    What  is  the  default  end  and  why ?  --->  -string length - 1  becoz  index  of  last  char  is  -length
