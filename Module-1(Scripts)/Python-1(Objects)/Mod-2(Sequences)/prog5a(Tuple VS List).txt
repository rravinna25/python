Tuple  Vs  List
-----------------
1) Can  tuple  be  modified ?  ---> No  becoz  it  is  an  immutable  object
    What  about  list ?  ---> It  can  be  modified  becoz  it  is  a  mutable  object

2) What  is  tuple  operator ?  ---> ( ) 
     What  is  list  operator ?  ---> [ ] 

3) (10 , 20 , 15 , 18) 
    Are  ()  mandatory  ?  --->  No  and  ()  are  optional
    [10 , 20 , 15 , 18]
    Are  []  mandatory  ?  --->  Yes

4) What  is  another  name  of  tuple ?  --->  Read-only  list  becoz  tuple  can  be  accessed  but  can  not  be  modified

5) Is  tuple  growable  and  shrinkable ?  ---> No  becoz  tuple  is  immutable
    What  about  list ?  --->  It  is  growable  and  shrinkable

6) Is  tuple  size  fixed  (or)  variable ?  ---> Fixed  size
    What  about  list ?  ---> Variable  size

7) Is  tuple . append(x)  valid ?  ---> No  becoz  there  is  no  append()  method  in  tuple
    What  does  list . append(x)  do ?  ---> Inserts  'x'  at  the  end  of  the  list

8) Is  tuple . remove(x)  valid ? ---> No  becoz  there  is  no  remove()  method  in  tuple
    What  does  list . remove(x)  do ? ---> Removes  first  'x'  from  the  list

9) How  is  tuple  of  single  element  denoted ?  --->  (element,)  and  ,  is  mandatory
     How  is  list  of  single  element  denoted ? ---> [element,]  and  ,  is  optional
